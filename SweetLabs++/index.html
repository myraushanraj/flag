Allergen-Free Delights: Savor the Joy

Welcome to Sweetlabs, the brainchild of the talented baker, Shriya. With a burning passion for baking and a deep understanding of the challenges faced by individuals with allergies to milk and eggs, Shriya embarked on a mission to revolutionize the world of allergy-friendly treats. Shriya's expertise and creativity shine through in every delectable creation. Her unwavering commitment to crafting cakes & cupcakes that are not only safe but also irresistibly delicious is the heart and soul of our business.
