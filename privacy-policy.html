1.) The Information We Collect and How We Use It

Flagship provides mentorship to students & it is an interactive e-commerce platform that allows users to create and curate online shops and sell products to their friends, family, and other Flagship users. We call registered users who have created these shops “Flagpreneur” Below we have summarized how and when we collect information to provide the Services and how we use it. The information we collect will in part depend on what type of user you are (i.e., Flagpreneur, parent, visitor, or buyer). Please note that if you choose not to share certain information with us, some features may not function as intended or at all. Information We Collect From All Users of the Services Whether you are a Flagpreneur, a buyer, or simply a visitor to the Services, we collect a variety of information about how you use and interact with the Services through the use of cookies or other online tools. This information includes IP address, the type and version of your device, information about your device’s operating system, and device identifiers. We also automatically collect information about how you use the Services, including content viewed, duration of use, what website you were using before you visited the Services, and similar information. We use this information to enable all users to use the Services and its features, analyze use and effectiveness of the Services, improve the Services and develop new features, maintain the security and integrity of the Services, ensure legal and regulatory compliance, personalize content, troubleshoot errors, and (with device permissions enabled) send user push notifications. The third-party service provider that assist us in collecting this information is Google Analytics. Please see those providers’ websites for more information about what they collect and how you can opt out. Please also note that you can exercise choice with respect to the collection of information through cookies through your browser settings. We may also collect basic contact information from you if you fill out a form to request additional information, submit an inquiry to customer service, or enroll in a contest or sweepstakes. The information provided in connection with these activities will be used to process your request and for our business purposes.

Information We Collect from Registered Flagpreneurs

We collect a variety of information from Flagpreneurs to provide the Services and facilitate the shop. This information includes:

Registration Information/Parent Contact Information

To register on the Services, you will be required to provide your name, email address, age, password, and a unique username. This information will be used by us to provide the Services to you, enhance the Services, secure the Services, and communicate with you, including by sending marketing communications. An adult who is the Flagpreneurs parent or legal guardian will be required to pay the applicable fee indicated on the Services to launch the shop and allow the Flagpreneurs to purchase products from vendors to sell in their shop, which will require entering a valid payment account number. The payor’s credit card information will be collected and stored by our payment processor, Stripe, and not by us. In accordance with applicable legal requirements, we may also collect the Flagpreneurs or a parent’s phone number to send SMS messages about the Services, as well as the parent’s email address to communicate with the parent about the child and the Services, including to send the parent information about promotions, contests, and marketing materials.

Shop and Profile Information.

In setting up your shop, you will be able to provide a cover image, draft text to introduce yourself and your shop to potential buyers and other users, view and select the products you want to sell, and provide descriptions of the products you are selling. You will also be required to provide email addresses of potential buyers so that you can contact them through the Services about your shop and the products you are selling. Please note that if the recipients of the emails you send through the Services unsubscribe from receiving future marketing communications from the Services, you will no longer be able to email those unsubscribed users though the Services. As a Flagpreneurs, you will also have the ability to interact with other users and Flagpreneurs to share information about your shop and offer feedback on the products you sell and your shop’s success, and the ability to participate in forums and group features. We use shop and profile information to facilitate the Services and your account, enhance the Services, analyze use of the Services, and to develop new features.

Information We Collect from Buyers

If you make a purchase on the Services, we will collect information needed to facilitate your purchase, including your name, email address, telephone number, and billing and shipping contact information. Your payment information will be collected by our third-party payment processor, Stripe, and not by us.

Aggregate/De-Identified data

We may aggregate and/or de-identify any information collected through the Services from all types of users so that such information can no longer be linked to you or your device (“Aggregate/De-Identified Information”). We may use Aggregate/De-Identified Information for any purpose, including without limitation for research and marketing purposes, and we may also share such information with any third parties, including with our partners.
