About Us:

Hello! My name is Prachi Mehrotra and I am the founder of this company, Layerd Dreams! We sell all types of stickers, layered stickers, designed cups and mugs, and designed t-shirts too! Don’t you just love something that is pretty and customized to you, but you never had the resources to make something like that yourself? That’s where we can help! Layered Dreams can take orders for what type of craft you want, and then send it to you with little cost! We can take orders to make any designed layered stickers, t-shirts, mugs, and more! I made this company because I love making crafts and giving them out so people can use them to make their living spaces more beautiful and customized to you. Worried about the planet? That’s fine too! Our products are also recyclable, so once they are too old you can just recycle them!
