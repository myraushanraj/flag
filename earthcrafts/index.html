About Us:

At EarthCrafts, we are passionate about celebrating the beauty and versatility of clay. Our team, guided by Sparsh Sunjea, meticulously handcraft each piece, infusing it with their creativity and love for the craft. We believe in promoting sustainable and eco-friendly practices, using natural clay and embracing the inherent beauty of imperfections.
