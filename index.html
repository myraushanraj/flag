Stats

The gruesome stats clinging on generation on generation has only reduced innovation and eradicated productive work across america. We are pretty proud to share that we are working on to improve these dire stats and help people become the next generation of leader.

Objectives

To empower and support the next generation of entrepreneurs by providing a platform for kid-led businesses, mentorship, peer-to-peer learning opportunities, and access to resources for academic and extracurricular development.

Vision

Create a community of young, innovative, and successful entrepreneurs who are equipped with the skills and resources to help them envision & achieve their goals and make a positive impact on the world.

Goals

To establish a successful and profitable marketplace for kid-led businesses.

To provide mentorship and resources for kid entrepreneurs to help them succeed.

To help students fund their businesses



4 Different Tranches

Ecommerce

World’s first ecommerce marketplace fostering next-generation of american entrepreneurs & providing practical entrepreneurial exposure.

Community Building

A dedicated community of students building and creating businesses and offering highly skilled freelance services.

Enterpreneurship

Online & On-site entrepreneurship bootcamp forging entrepreneurship among students through curriculum designed by industry leaders & school experts.

Mentorship

Industry leaders & top academic experts get together to create a curriculum that places the founder hat on students and help in development of creative as well as analytical skills.
